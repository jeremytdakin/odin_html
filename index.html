Ice Cream Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3c Heavy Cream
  • 1.5c Whole Milk
  • 1.5c Allulose
  • 1/4 tsp xanthan gum
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a stand mixer and mix on low speed for 2 minutes.
  2. Churn until firm.
  3. Enjoy!
